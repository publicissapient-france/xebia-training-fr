Kanban & BDD 2 days

Differentiation, Complexity & Cynefin (Discussion, Workshop)

Every project involves doing something new; something that we’ve never done before.

In this session we see if we can identify the differentiators for our companies and projects. These are the places where requirements will be harder to understand and implement, and where practices which allow us to respond to discoveries are essential.

Cynefin is a framework for helping us work out if we are dealing with complexity or if work is merely complicated, and to consider different approaches for each.
