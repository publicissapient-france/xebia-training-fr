Xavier Quesada (Coach Agile et Certified Scrum Trainer)

Xavier is an independent consultant, coach, trainer, project manager and author, specializing in applying Lean-Agile-Scrum methods to create high performance, continuously improving teams and organizations. He is a Certified Scrum Coach & Trainer and the author of the Visual Management Blog.

As a community leader and frequent international conference speaker, he is passionate about promoting lean and agile methodologies as a more productive and more sustainable way of working.
