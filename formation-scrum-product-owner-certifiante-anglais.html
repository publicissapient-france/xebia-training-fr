Présentation

This two-day Scrum Product Owner Certification course provides the jumping off point for mastering the most difficult role in Scrum. Being an effective Scrum Product Owner is difficult, but, if executed well, can be incredibly rewarding.

During the two days of scrum training, you’ll learn Scrum and Agile fundamentals along with invaluable skills such as how to interact with the development team, articulate a clear vision with measurable business objectives, maximize ROI within the context of product plans, and track progress. You'll also learn the differences between agile processes and traditional "waterfall" methodologies, as well as how to plan releases and sprints and scale Scrum to program-level efforts.

Upon completion, participants earn their Scrum Product Owner Certification, which includes a two-year membership to the Scrum Alliance, which offers valuable materials and information to Product Owners.
